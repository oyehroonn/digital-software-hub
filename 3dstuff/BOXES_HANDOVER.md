# DSM product-box handover

## What renders on the site

- The storefront uses `@google/model-viewer` through `src/components/ProductModelViewer.tsx`.
- Production GLBs are served by `https://dsm-api.techrealm.ai` from the VPS (`/root/3dstuff/models/`).
- Catalogue cards use the GLBs in `/models/`; a live API outage falls back to the card icon rather than blocking the UI.

## Geometry contract

`batch_process.py` is the single geometry source for catalogue and DSM-original boxes. Keep:

```python
BOX_W = 1.5
BOX_H = 2.1
BOX_D = 0.22
```

The 0.22 depth is intentional: software packaging should look like a thin carton, not a deep retail cube. `gen_own_boxes.py` imports the same `apply_texture`, so do **not** implement a second box mesh there. Creative archives map their `Front` art to the front, their matching `Back` art (including the quote/text) to the rear face, and a numbered `Spine.png` to the right side and mirrored left side. Never put rear artwork behind a catalogue card or replace approved spine art with a flat edge colour.

## Regenerating safely

1. Regenerate locally first and visually inspect a representative Microsoft, Autodesk, Adobe and DSM-original GLB.
2. Copy the reviewed generator to the VPS and regenerate `/root/3dstuff/models/` there.
3. Keep front/back cover UVs upright; use the sampled edge colour for side/top/bottom faces.
4. Verify the live `dsm-api.techrealm.ai/models/.../model.glb` URLs before frontend deployment.

## Registered creative boxes

The supplied Creative Studio source archives are extracted under `3dstuff/creative-boxes/`:

- `autodesk/` — Autodesk 2027 package artwork, including the AEC Collection.
- `microsoft/` — Office, Windows, Dynamics and Microsoft 365 package artwork.

`import_creative_boxes.py` converts the approved front artwork into thin catalogue GLBs. Its imported archive-backed models begin at `99001`; `creative-manifest.json` on the VPS records the current collection. `src/data/dsmChoices.ts` defines the curated eight used at the top of the catalogue, the Digital Software Market Choice shelf, and the Autodesk feature.

The original DSM-created models remain `90001` through `90013`, listed in `src/data/ownProducts.ts` and generated by `gen_own_boxes.py`. The `/creatives` gallery fetches and displays all registered archive-backed creative models first, with a curated fallback if the API is temporarily unavailable.

When registering a new creative box, record its approved source file, creator credit, product URL, consent/usage status and assigned model id before adding it to the gallery. Never invent creator names or claim that third-party vendor art was created by DSM.
